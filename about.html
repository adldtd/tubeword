About TubeWord


TubeWord uses a database of Youtube links in order to retrieve links with words in them. Therefore, some words (especially ones with more than six letters) may not give back results. Currently, all 2-4 letter English words have at least one link documented alongside them. As more work is completed, this number is likely to change. As well as this, a number of Youtube links may be broken.

The database utilizes a modified version of the dwyl english-words text file as its "dictionary"; it can be found at https://github.com/dwyl/english-words

To request the removal of a Youtube link, or to check if a Youtube link is in the database, email PLACEHOLDER@GMAIL.COM
